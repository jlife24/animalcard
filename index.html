Cheetah

Due to the energy required to run at such speed, cheetahs can only maintain a chase for between 200 - 300m. If a hunt isn't successful after 1 min, they usually stop to rest.

  • Location: sub-Saharan Africa, eastern and southern Africa, southern Algeria, northern Niger and Iran
  • Size: 112-150 cm (45 - 60 in)
  • Weight: 46 - 159 pounds (21 - 72 kg)
  • Habitat: Dry Grasslands, Scrub Forests & Savannahs
  • Top speed: 100 kph (65 mph)

The Cheetah is the fastest land animal on the planet. They are members of the family Felidae, which includes the tiger, lion, jaguar, leopard, snow leopard, and other big cats.
